CancellationException(message: String?)

Platform and version requirements: JS, Native

Thrown by cancellable suspending functions if the Job of the coroutine is cancelled while it is suspending. It indicates normal cancellation of a coroutine. It is not printed to console/log by default uncaught exception handler. (see handleCoroutineException).
