FlowPreview()

Marks all Flow declarations as a feature preview to indicate that Flow is still experimental and has a ‘preview’ status.

Flow preview has no backward compatibility guarantees, including both binary and source compatibility. Its API and semantics can and will be changed in next releases.

Feature preview can be used to evaluate its real-world strengths and weaknesses, gather and provide feedback. According to the feedback, Flow will be refined on its road to stabilization and promotion to stable API.
